Welcome to New America Foundation

New America Foundation offers world-class, affordable education in Pakistan. Established under the aegis of The City School, it is committed to providing an efficient learning environment for your children, helping them compete and excel in the rapidly evolving environment of today. We offer quality education from kindergarten till Matriculation, in efficient and well-organised campuses.
